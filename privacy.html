Privacy Policy

Effective: June 15, 2026

1. What We Collect

When you purchase a plan, we collect your email address (to deliver your API key and receipts). We do not require registration or an account to use the service.

When you make an API call, we log the following for operational purposes only:

  • Your API key identifier (not the full key)
  • The model requested
  • Token count
  • Timestamp and anonymized IP address

We do not log, store, or inspect the content of your API requests (messages/prompts). All request data is forwarded to DeepSeek's API and is not retained on our servers beyond the duration of the request.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To deliver API keys and transaction receipts via email
  • To calculate usage and enforce rate limits
  • To improve service reliability and detect abuse

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We do not train AI models on your data.

3. Data Retention

Usage logs are retained for 90 days. Payment records are retained as required by applicable financial regulations. You may request deletion of your data by contacting contact@tb-api.top.

4. Third-Party Services

TokenBridge uses the following third-party services:

  • DeepSeek API — to process AI model requests. Your messages are forwarded to DeepSeek for inference. See DeepSeek's privacy policy.
  • SendGrid / 163 SMTP — to send transactional emails (API keys, receipts).
  • Cloudflare — CDN and DDoS protection. Cloudflare may process minimal connection metadata.

5. Cookies

TokenBridge does not use tracking cookies. We may use essential session cookies for administrative functions only.

6. Contact

For privacy inquiries: contact@tb-api.top

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.
